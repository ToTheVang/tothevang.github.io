WiFi AR Scanner

Privacy Policy | Last Updated: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ToTheVang ("we", "us", or "our") manages user information for the WiFi AR Scanner mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

WiFi AR Scanner is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as your name, email address, or account data to our servers.

2. App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Location (Fine & Coarse): Required by Android to scan nearby WiFi networks. Location data is used only on-device to display network names and signal directions. It is never transmitted to external servers.
  • Camera: Used for the AR Camera view to overlay WiFi network information on your live camera feed. Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored or transmitted.

No data gathered through these permissions is sent to us or any third party beyond ad services described below.

3. Data Storage

All app-related data, including WiFi scan results and your settings, are processed and stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to this data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.
